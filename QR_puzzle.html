Holodeck Admin Console — Access Log

Once the isolinear circuit is unscrambled, you are able to log into the holodeck’s admin console. From there you learn that from 1300 until 1460 the holodeck was in use.

Doing some digging you learn that the program being ran was one on the “restricted program list” — “Two Klingons. one bat'leth”. The voice command override was none other than Commander Nira Vess. You find him sitting in one of the station’s ready rooms where you confront him.

“Oh, uh...that program was...uh, nothing more than research. I was trying to understand why anyone would want to run that program in the first place. I..uh, look - can we please just drop this? We have other more pressing matters at hand such as the Tandaran fleet. I trust you are making progress on the murder?

If you will excuse me, I have to go see about a bat'leth... I mean uh a bathroom. Goodbye.”
